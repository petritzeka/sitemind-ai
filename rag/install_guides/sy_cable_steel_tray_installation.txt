TITLE: Installing SY Cable on Steel Tray

OVERVIEW:
SY cable is flexible with braided armour. This guide covers routing it on tray, avoiding damage to braid and terminating correctly.

TOOLS / PPE:
- Cutters
- Screwdrivers
- Measuring tape
- Cable ties
- PPE

MATERIALS:
- SY cable
- SY glands
- Steel tray
- Isolator/outlet
- Fixings

SAFETY CHECKS:
- Avoid overtightening cable ties (crushes braid).
- Maintain bend radius.

METHOD:
1. Mount steel tray in the required route.
2. Lay SY cable along tray and secure lightly.
3. Avoid sharp edges that may damage the braid.
4. Prepare termination:
   - Strip outer sheath cleanly.
   - Expose braid without cutting strands.
   - Fit SY gland correctly.
5. Terminate into isolator/outlet ensuring CPC continuity.

GOOD PRACTICE:
- Keep braid intact.
- No excessive bending.
