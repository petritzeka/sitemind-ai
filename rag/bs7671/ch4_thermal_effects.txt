BS 7671 Chapter 4 – Protection Against Thermal Effects

Purpose:
• Prevent risk of fire, burns, or overheating in installations.

Key requirements:
• Cables must be protected from external heat sources.
• Equipment must not exceed temperature limits.
• Items installed near combustible materials require special consideration.
• Arcing and sparking must be prevented.
• Luminaires must be suitable for their environment.

Common examples:
• Downlights require correct fire-rated fittings.
• Cables in insulation must use correction factor Ci.
