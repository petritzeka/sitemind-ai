BS 7671 Chapter 1 – Scope, Object and Fundamental Principles

Scope:
• Covers all fixed electrical installations in the UK.
• Applies to LV and ELV systems (up to 1000V AC).

Object of the regulations:
• Ensure safety from electric shock.
• Protect against fire, heat, and mechanical damage.
• Provide correct functionality of the installation.

Fundamental principles:
• Protection against shock: basic protection + fault protection.
• Protection against thermal effects.
• Selection of appropriate equipment.
• Adequate maintenance and safe working practices.

Key definitions:
• Live part, exposed-conductive-part, fault current, SELV, PELV, FELV.
