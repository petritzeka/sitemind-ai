Design sequence:
1. Determine supply characteristics (TN-S, TN-C-S, TT).
2. Assess maximum demand + diversity.
3. Select protective devices (BS EN 60898, BS 88).
4. Size cables using Ib ≤ In ≤ Iz.
5. Check correction factors: Ca, Ci, Cg.
6. Check volt drop: 3% lighting, 5% power.
7. Check fault protection: Zs ≤ max Zs.
8. Check thermal withstand (adiabatic equation).
9. Select containment and routing.
10. Produce drawings and documentation.

Volt drop calculation:
VD = (mV/A/m × Ib × length) ÷ 1000

Adiabatic equation:
S ≥ √(I²t / k²)

Main considerations:
• Special locations.
• RCD requirements.
• Coordination of protective devices.
• Shock protection (ADS, SELV, PELV).
