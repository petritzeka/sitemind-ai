Preparation:
• Read drawings and specs.
• Select tools and materials.
• Complete RAMS.

Installation skills:
• Cable pulling, bending, dressing.
• Conduit/trunking/basket installation.
• Tray and SWA glanding.
• Support spacing to prevent premature collapse.
• Maintain segregation of LV/ELV.

Connections and terminations:
• Torque settings.
• Correct tightening sequence.
• Ferrules for stranded conductors.

Pre-commissioning:
• Visual checks.
• Ensure correct identification and labelling.
• Verify CPC continuity before energising.
