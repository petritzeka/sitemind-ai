Typical Full Timeline

According to the LEC Gold Card pathway:
• Level 2 Diploma: 4–5 months
• Level 3 Diploma: 5 months
• 18th Edition: 1 week (can be done anytime during Level 2/3)
• NVQ + AM2: 5–12 months
• Inspection & Testing (2391-type): ~1 month part-time (optional but highly recommended) 

Total realistic time:
12–24 months depending on work experience and portfolio speed.
