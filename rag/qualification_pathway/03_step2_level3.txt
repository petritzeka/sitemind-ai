Step 2 — Level 3 Diploma (5 months)

• You must have the Level 2 Diploma certificate before starting.
• Experience is not required, but working as an electrician’s mate is recommended.
• Mix of theory and practical lessons.
• Typical duration at LEC: 5 months (Fast Track).
• Multiple day/evening timetable options exist (Mon evening, Tue/Wed/Thu daytime). 

Completing Level 3 is essential before starting your NVQ portfolio.
