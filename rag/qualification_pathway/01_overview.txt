Overview of the UK Gold Card Route

To become a fully qualified Installation Electrician in the UK, you must complete four key steps:

Level 2 Diploma in Electrical Installations

Level 3 Diploma in Electrical Installations

18th Edition Wiring Regulations

NVQ Level 3 + AM2 assessment

Once these are completed, you receive the ECS Gold Card (Installation Electrician).
This pathway is shown clearly in the LEC “Gold Card Steps” diagram, which lists the same 4 steps and timelines:
• Level 2: 4–5 months
• Level 3: 5 months
• NVQ + AM2: 5–12 months
• 18th Edition + Testing: short courses alongside the main route
