Earth Fault Loop Impedance (Zs & Ze)

Ze (external earth fault loop impedance):
• Measured at origin with main switch OFF
• Helps determine maximum Zs values

Zs (earth fault loop impedance at a point):
• Zs = Ze + (R1 + R2)
• Must be below the maximum permitted value in BS 7671 for the protective device.

Low Zs ensures fast disconnection of supply in a fault.
