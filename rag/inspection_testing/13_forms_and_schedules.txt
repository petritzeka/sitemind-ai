Forms, Certificates, and Schedules

Electricians must know how to complete:

• Electrical Installation Certificate (EIC)
• Minor Works Certificate
• Electrical Installation Condition Report (EICR)
• Schedule of Test Results
• Schedule of Inspections

Common values recorded:
• R1+R2
• Ring continuity r1/rn/r2
• IR values
• Ze and Zs
• RCD times
• PSC/PEFC

Correct, legible documentation is part of BS 7671 compliance.
