Electrical Installation Certificate – Structure Overview

Sections:

1. Details of Client and Installation
• Names, address, type of installation (new/addition/alteration).

2. Description and Extent of Installation
• Clear summary of what the work covers.

3. Supply Characteristics and Earthing Arrangements
• TN-S, TN-C-S, TT.
• Supply voltage, frequency, PFC, Ze.
• Main protective device rating.

4. Particulars of Installation Referred to in the Certificate
• Maximum demand.
• Earthing conductor CSA.
• Bonding conductor sizes.
• Main switch type and rating.

5. Schedule of Inspections
• Visual checks: intake, ADS, segregation, identification, IP rating.

6. Schedule of Test Results
• R1+R2 or R2, IR, polarity, Zs, RCD tests.

7. Signatures
• Design, construction, inspection & testing.
• Dates and names must be present.
