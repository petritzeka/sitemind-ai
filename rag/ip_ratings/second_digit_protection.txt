Second Digit (Protection Against Liquids):

0 – No protection  
1 – Dripping water  
2 – Drips at 15 degrees  
3 – Sprays up to 60 degrees  
4 – Splashing water  
5 – Water jets  
6 – Powerful jets  
7 – Immersion up to 1m  
8 – Continuous immersion  
9 – High-pressure hot water (industrial)

Common domestic examples:
- IP44 (bathroom zone 2 fittings)
- IP65 (outdoor lights)
- IP67 (garden ground lighting)
