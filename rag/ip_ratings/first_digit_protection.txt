First Digit (Protection Against Solids):

0 – No protection  
1 – Large objects >50mm  
2 – Fingers >12.5mm  
3 – Tools/wires >2.5mm  
4 – Tools/wires >1mm  
5 – Dust protected  
6 – Dust tight (complete protection)

Most common in domestic:
- IP2X for consumer units (prevent finger contact)
- IP4X required on top of consumer units (prevent wires entering)
