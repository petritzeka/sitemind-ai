Supply systems:
• TN-S: separate earth/neutral.
• TN-C-S: PME.
• TT: earth rod, RCD is main protection.

Circuit types:
• Ring final circuits (2.5mm² + 32A).
• Radials (2.5mm² 20A, or 4mm² 32A).
• Lighting circuits (1.0/1.5mm² + 6A).
• Cooker circuits (6mm² + 32-45A).

Protective devices:
• MCB types: B (domestic), C (commercial), D (industrial).
• Fuses: BS1361, BS3036, BS88.

RCD rules:
• 30 mA for sockets, domestic circuits, bathrooms, outdoors.

Cable selection:
• Ib ≤ In ≤ Iz.
• Apply correction factors: Ca, Ci, Cg.
• Volt drop limits: 3% lighting, 5% other.

Earthing & bonding:
• Main bonding (10mm² copper typical).
• Supplementary bonding only where required.

Enclosures:
• IP ratings: IP4X DBs, IPX4 bathrooms.

BS7671 design steps:
1. Determine demand.
2. Select protective device.
3. Select cable size.
4. Check volt drop.
5. Check Zs.
6. Documentation.
