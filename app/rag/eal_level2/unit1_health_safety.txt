Purpose of H&S:
• Prevent injury, electric shock, fire, and accidents.
• Follow legislation: HASAWA 1974, EAWR 1989.

Risk assessments:
• Identify hazards.
• Who may be harmed.
• Evaluate risk.
• Control measures.
• Review regularly.

Common hazards:
• Working live.
• Working at height.
• Trip hazards.
• Damaged tools.
• Poor lighting or ventilation.

PPE:
• Safety boots, gloves, goggles, arc-rated clothing.

Safe isolation procedure:
1. Identify circuit.
2. Prove tester on known live source.
3. Isolate supply using correct device.
4. Lock off + warning tag.
5. Prove dead.
6. Re-prove tester.

Fire safety:
• Extinguishers: CO₂ (electrical), powder, water (non-electrical).
• Call emergency services.
• Evacuate safely.

Manual handling:
• Straight back, bend knees.
• Use assistance for heavy loads.
