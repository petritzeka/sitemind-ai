Live Tests – Sequence and Purpose (BS 7671)

1. Polarity (live)
• Confirm line is truly line.
• Single phase: 230 V L–N, 230 V L–E, 0 V N–E.
• Three phase: check L1/L2/L3 phase sequence.

2. Ze – External Earth Fault Loop Impedance
• Test with supply on but installation isolated.
• Typical values:
  – TN-C-S: ~0.35 Ω
  – TN-S: ~0.8 Ω
  – TT: up to 21 Ω

3. Prospective Fault Current (PFC)
• Highest of PSCC/PEFC.
• Use meter auto-mode or calculate using Ohm’s Law (I = V / Z).

4. Zs – Earth Fault Loop Impedance of Circuit
• Zs = Ze + (R1 + R2)
• Must be below max allowed for protective device.
• 0.4 s disconnection for ≤32A final circuits.

5. RCD Tests
• ½ × IΔn – should NOT trip.
• 1 × IΔn – trip within 300 ms.
• 5 × IΔn – rapid trip (40 ms typical).
• Test button must operate.

6. Functional Testing
• Check switches, isolators, equipment function.
• Check correct labelling and identification.
