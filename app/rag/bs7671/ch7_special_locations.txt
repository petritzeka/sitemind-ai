BS 7671 Chapter 7 – Special Installations and Locations

Covers special areas with higher risk:
• Bathrooms (Section 701)
• Swimming pools (702)
• Hot tubs and spas
• Agricultural locations (705)
• Marinas and boatyards (709)
• Caravans (721)
• EV charging installations (722)
• Solar PV systems (712)

Bathrooms:
• Zones: 0, 1, 2.
• RCD 30 mA required.
• IPX4 or higher depending on zone.

EV charge points:
• RCD Types A or B depending on charger.
• PME considerations (open PEN protection).

PV systems:
• DC isolation, correct cable selection, earthing arrangements.

Key principle:
• Additional measures to reduce risk in special environments.
