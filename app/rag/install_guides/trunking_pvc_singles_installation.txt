TITLE: Installing PVC Trunking with Singles

OVERVIEW:
PVC trunking is used to protect and organise single-core cables. This guide covers mounting trunking, using bends and routing singles neatly.

TOOLS / PPE:
- Saw
- Screwdrivers
- File/reamer
- Tape measure
- PPE

MATERIALS:
- PVC trunking & lid
- T-bends, couplers, end caps
- 2.5 mmÂ² singles
- Back box
- Fuse spur

SAFETY CHECKS:
- Deburr all cut edges.
- Do not overload trunking.

METHOD:
1. Mark the route and fix trunking level.
2. Cut trunking to length and deburr edges.
3. Fit bends, couplers and end caps.
4. Lay singles inside trunking neatly without twists.
5. Install lid securely.
6. Terminate into accessories.

GOOD PRACTICE:
- Even spacing of screws.
- Wires laid flat and organised.
