TITLE: Installing Steel Conduit with PVC Singles

OVERVIEW:
Steel conduit provides excellent mechanical protection. This guide covers bending, joining and pulling singles for multi-way switching.

TOOLS / PPE:
- Conduit bender
- Hacksaw
- Reamer/file
- Screwdrivers
- Pulling cord
- PPE

MATERIALS:
- 20 mm steel conduit
- Couplers and bushes
- Back boxes
- PVC singles (line, neutral, CPC, strappers)
- Ceiling roses or light fittings

SAFETY CHECKS:
- Deburr all conduit ends.
- Fit bushes to prevent insulation damage.

METHOD:
1. Plan conduit route and measurements.
2. Bend conduit using correct method and radius.
3. Join conduit with couplers and secure bushings.
4. Fix conduit level and secure.
5. Pull singles through using draw wire.
6. Terminate at switch (COM, L1, L2), intermediate switch if used, and light points.

GOOD PRACTICE:
- No sharp edges inside conduit.
- Use lubrication for long runs.
