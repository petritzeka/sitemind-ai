TITLE: Installing PVC Conduit with Singles and FP200

OVERVIEW:
PVC conduit is used to protect cables on surface installations. This guide covers bending PVC conduit, pulling singles and routing FP200 safely.

TOOLS / PPE:
- Pipe cutter
- Screwdrivers
- Bending spring
- Level
- Tape measure
- PPE

MATERIALS:
- 20 mm PVC conduit
- Couplers and bushes
- Back boxes
- 1.5 or 2.5 mmÂ² singles
- FP200 cable
- Female adapters

SAFETY CHECKS:
- Check bends are smooth without kinks.
- Avoid forcing FP200 around tight bends.

METHOD:
1. Measure and cut conduit.
2. Bend using a bending spring for smooth curves.
3. Fit couplers and bushes where necessary.
4. Secure conduit onto wall/board.
5. Pull singles through carefully.
6. Run FP200 separately where required and secure.
7. Terminate into accessories.

GOOD PRACTICE:
- Smooth, neat bends.
- No insulation damage.
