FAQs

Do I need experience before Level 2?
No — Level 2 is designed for complete beginners. 

Can I work while studying?
Yes — many students work as mates during Level 2/3.

When should I start the NVQ?
After finishing Level 3.

How long does NVQ + AM2 take?
Typically 5–12 months depending on how fast you gather evidence. 

When do I get the Gold Card?
After completing all four steps.

Do I need Inspection & Testing?
Not required for the Gold Card, but strongly recommended for better employability.
