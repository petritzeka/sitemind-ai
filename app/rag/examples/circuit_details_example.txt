Example – Circuit Details Page (Typical Domestic Board)

DB reference: DB1
Location: Hall cupboard
Supply: TN-C-S, Ze = 0.35 Ω
Main switch: 100 A DP isolator

Circuit 1 – Ring final sockets
• Cable: 2.5 mm² T&E
• CPC: 1.5 mm²
• Reference method: 100 (in wall)
• Protective device: B32 MCB
• Max Zs permitted: 1.37 Ω
• RCD: 30 mA, Type A

Circuit 2 – Upstairs lights
• Cable: 1.0 mm² T&E
• CPC: 1.0 mm²
• Protective device: B6 MCB
• Max Zs permitted: 7.67 Ω
• No RCD (if pre-18th), 30 mA if new

Circuit 3 – Cooker
• Cable: 6.0 mm² T&E
• Protective device: B32 MCB
• Max Zs permitted: 1.37 Ω
• RCD: 30 mA

Circuit 4 – Shower
• Cable: 10 mm² T&E
• Protective device: B40 MCB
• Max Zs permitted: 1.16 Ω
• RCD: 30 mA

Notes:
• Each circuit includes: cable size, reference method, device type/rating, max Zs, RCD data.
