Old-Style Fuse Boards (rewireable fuses)

Characteristics:
- BS3036 fuses, sometimes cartridge style.
- Labels are often handwritten or very faded.
- Many boards have missing or incorrect labels.
- Frequently found in old domestic properties.

Common problems:
- Circuits not numbered.
- Circuits labelled "sockets" with no indication of floor.
- Shared neutral issues.

OCR Notes:
These boards require more interpretation. Use common circuit patterns + property type assumptions to build the table.
