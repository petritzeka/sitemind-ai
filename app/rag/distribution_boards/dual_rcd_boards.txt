Dual RCD Boards (split-load consumer units)

Characteristics:
- Two RCDs: Left RCD and Right RCD.
- Circuits are grouped under each RCD.
- First module often main switch, then RCD, then MCBs.

OCR Challenges:
- Sometimes labels are written only once for each group.
- Circuits may be labelled with arrows or grouped descriptions.

Common circuits:
Left side:
- Lights up
- Lights down
- Cooker
- Smoke alarms

Right side:
- Sockets ring
- Kitchen ring
- Boiler
- Shower

Conversion tip:
Match circuit numbers to positions under each RCD, not top-to-bottom sequentially.
