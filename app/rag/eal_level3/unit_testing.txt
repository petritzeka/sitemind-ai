Inspection (before testing):
• Verify presence of bonding.
• Check IP rating.
• Check correct cable routing.
• Check identification and labelling.
• Check accessibility of equipment.

Testing – dead tests:
1. CPC continuity (R1+R2).
2. Ring final continuity (r1, rn, r2).
3. Insulation resistance >1 MΩ.
4. Polarity.

Testing – live tests:
1. Ze.
2. Zs.
3. PSCC/PEFC.
4. RCD tests: ½×, 1×, 5×.
5. Functional tests.

Certification:
• EIC, EICR, Minor Works.
• Schedules of inspections & test results.

Commissioning:
• Confirm operation of all equipment.
• Hand over documentation.
