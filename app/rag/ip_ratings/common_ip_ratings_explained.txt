IP20:
- No water protection.
- Common for indoor accessories (sockets, switches).

IP44:
- Splash proof.
- Used in bathrooms (zone 2) and sheltered outdoor areas.

IP55:
- Jet resistant.
- Suitable for some outside environments.

IP65:
- Dust tight + water jets.
- Common for outdoor wall lights.

IP66:
- Powerful water jets.
- Harsh outdoor environments.

IP67:
- Submersible up to 1m.
- Garden lighting, ground spike lights.

IP68:
- Continuous submersion.
- Ponds, water features.
