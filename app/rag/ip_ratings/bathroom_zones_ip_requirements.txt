Bathroom zones (BS 7671 & IET guidance):

Zone 0:
- Inside bath/shower.
- Minimum IPX7.
- SELV only (12V max).

Zone 1:
- Above bath/shower up to 2.25m.
- Minimum IPX4.
- IPX5 if water jets possible.

Zone 2:
- 0.6m around bath/shower.
- Minimum IPX4.

Outside zones:
- No minimum requirement, but IP44+ recommended.

Note:
Shaver sockets, pull cords, and extractor fans have special conditions.
